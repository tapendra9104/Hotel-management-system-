Our Story

A Legacy of Luxury

Nestled in the heart of the city, GrandStay has been the epitome of luxury hospitality for over two decades. Our commitment to excellence, attention to every detail, and passion for creating unforgettable experiences has made us a landmark destination for discerning travelers from around the world.

With 9 meticulously designed rooms ranging from cozy standard accommodations to opulent suites, a world-class spa, award-winning restaurant, and a team dedicated to your every need — GrandStay isn't just a hotel, it's an experience.

Every corner of our property reflects our dedication to elegance and comfort. From our grand marble lobby to our serene garden spaces, from our state-of-the-art fitness facilities to our rejuvenating spa, GrandStay is designed to exceed your expectations at every turn.
